
--- CROMOZOM ---
- Un cromozom = o permutare de indici de zboruri [0..N-1]
- Ordinea din cromozom = ordinea în care SchedulingEngine procesează zborurile
- SchedulingEngine calculează timpii reali; GA doar optimizează ordinea

--- POPULATIE INITIALA ---
- chromosome[0]       = ordinea greedy (earliest first, priority descending)
- chromosome[1..N/2]  = greedy + 10% adjacent swaps (variatie mica)
- chromosome[N/2..N]  = Fisher-Yates shuffle complet random

--- SELECTIE ---
- Tournament Selection: alege K cromozomi random, câstigă cel cu fitness minim
- K = TournamentSize (ex: 3-5)

--- INCRUCISARE (OX — Order Crossover) ---
- Probabilitate: CrossoverRate (ex: 0.9)
- Copiaza un segment random din parent1
- Restul pozitiilor se umplu in ordinea din parent2, sarand duplicatele
- Garanteaza ca fiecare zbor apare exact o data

--- MUTATII ---
1. MutateLocalSwap (probabilitate per gena: MutationRateLocal, ex: 0.005)
   - Swap intre zborul curent si un alt zbor cu scheduledTime in aceeasi fereastra de timp

2. MutateMemetic (probabilitate per cromozom: MutationRateMemetic, ex: 0.01)
   - Evalueaza cromozomul
   - Imparte scenariul in TimeWindows (ferestre fixe de timp)
   - Roulette wheel: alege fereastra cu probleme (penalitate mare = sanse mari)
   - ResolveIntensity: top 5% → ia 3 zboruri, top 10% → 2, altfel → 1
   - Pool = cele mai penalizate zboruri din fereastra + toate canceled din intreg cromozomul
   - Sorteaza pool dupa ReschedulingPriority (vezi formula jos)
   - Pool-ul merge primul in cromozomul nou → SchedulingEngine le asigneaza primele
   - Accepta schimbarea doar daca fitness-ul se imbunatateste

--- ELITISM ---
- Top EliteCount cromozomi trec direct in generatia urmatoare (neschiimbati)
- Garanteaza ca cel mai bun rezultat nu se pierde niciodata

--- EARLY STOPPING ---
- Daca generationsWithoutImprovement >= NoImprovementGenerations → oprire anticipata
- Se pastreaza mereu cel mai bun cromozom vazut vreodata

--- CP-SAT MICRO (rafinare exacta, frecventa) ---
- La fiecare CpSatMicroEveryNGenerations generatii
- Pe top CpSatEliteCount cromozomi + CpSatRandomCount cromozomi random
- 1 fereastra, CpSatNeighborhoodSize zboruri, 60ms limita de timp
- CP-SAT gaseste ordinea optima exacta in acel neighborhood mic

--- CP-SAT MACRO (rafinare exacta, mai rara) ---
- La fiecare CpSatMacroEveryNGenerations generatii
- Doar pe cel mai bun cromozom
- CpSatMacroWindowCount ferestre, 2x neighborhood size, 150ms limita

====================================================

--- FITNESS (penalitate totala, mai mic = mai bun) ---
  fitness = SUM peste toate zborurile de:
    - canceled:  180 * 1.2^(priority-1)
    - delayed:   delayMinutes * 1.2^(priority-1)
    - early:     earlyMinutes * 0.5 * 1.2^(priority-1)

--- PRIORITY MULTIPLIER ---
  multiplier = 1.2^(priority-1)
  priority 1 → 1.0x
  priority 2 → 1.2x
  priority 3 → 1.44x

--- RESCHEDULING PRIORITY (sorteaza pool-ul in MutateMemetic) ---
  priority = Alpha * penalty + Beta * (1 / (maxDelay + 1)) - Gamma * (maxEarly + maxDelay)
  Alpha = 1.0   (penalitate curenta)
  Beta  = 100.0 (urgenta: zbor cu maxDelay mic = mai urgent)
  Gamma = 0.01  (flexibilitate: fereastra mare = usor mai putin urgent)

--- SLOT SCORE (alege cea mai buna pista pentru un zbor in SchedulingEngine) ---
  early:   (1.0 + earlyMinutes * 0.5) * multiplier
  delayed: delayMinutes * multiplier

--- SEPARARE INTRE ZBORURI ---
  BaseSeparationSeconds * weatherMultiplier * eventMultiplier
  Weather:  Clear=1.0, Cloud=1.2, Rain=1.3, Snow=1.5, Fog=1.75, Storm=2.1
  Event partial (impact%): multiplier = 1 / (1 - impact/100)
  Event total (100%):      blocheaza pista complet — zborul e impins dupa eveniment

--- CP-SAT: FORMULA MATEMATICA ---
  Minimize:
    SUM pentru fiecare zbor i din neighborhood:
      delay_i * 2 * round(1.2^(priority_i - 1) * 100)
    + early_i * 1 * round(1.2^(priority_i - 1) * 100)
    + cancel_penalty_i

  unde:
    delay_i          = max(0, assignedTime_i - scheduledTime_i)   [secunde]
    early_i          = max(0, scheduledTime_i - assignedTime_i)   [secunde]
    cancel_penalty_i = 180 * 60 * 2 * round(1.2^(priority_i-1) * 100)  daca cancelat, altfel 0

  Subject to:
    assignedTime_i in [scheduledTime_i - maxEarly_i, scheduledTime_i + maxDelay_i]
    assignedTime_i + separationSec <= assignedTime_j   (i si j pe aceeasi pista, i inaintea lui j)
    assignedTime_i >= lastRunwayTime_runway + separationSec  (fata de zborurile fixe dinainte)
    exact o pista compatibila per zbor scheduled
